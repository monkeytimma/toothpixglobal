Our Team

Dr Aniruddha K B

An entrepreneur by passion and a dental surgeon by profession, Dr Aniruddha K B is committed to the cause of dental health and its significance in Global health scenario. He started Toothpix Dental Healthcare as a dedicated centre to providing quality dental healthcare at affordable costs, and to enhance dental health and its increased awareness among people. He developed a team of excellent healthcare professionals who are highly qualified and experienced to provide world-class dental treatments. Highly able, confident and friendly, this doctor definitely knows how to put you at ease before you begin your treatment.

Born and brought up amidst the serene landscapes of Coorg, Dr Aniruddha K B graduated from A B Shetty Memorial Institute of Dental sciences and completed his Masters at the ICR Institute of Clinical Sciences in the Republic of Singapore; He also has a Masters degree from University of Manchester, United Kingdom.

He has published papers in the areas of Clinical Integration and Global Health. He is also a resource person and visiting faculty in the areas of Global Health, Infectious Diseases and Chronic Disease Management at Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, India.

When this busy doctor finds some free time, he involves himself in painting and indulges in his keen interest in Indian Classical dance and music.

Dr Pavitra S Bhat

Ever smiling and confident, Dr.Pavitra S Bhat, instils a sense of confidence and comfort in all her patients, as well as her team. She is a general practitioner who specialises in Oral Surgery.A native of Mangalore, she graduated from K V G Institute of Dental Sciences and has worked in remote parts of southern Karnataka. She has also completed her specialised course in Implantology, and is a part of the Implant team at Toothpix Dental Healthcare.

While her excellent surgical skills are a tremendous asset toToothpix Dental Healthcare, her charming personality and the confidence she inspires in people has been invaluable to the growth of the clinic.

This beautiful doctor also plays the violin in her free time, and is a lover of Carnatic Classical Music.

Dr Shivaram Panya

Dr.Shivaram Panya, a highly qualified and dedicated Periodontal Surgeon and Implantologist is dedicated to the cause of improving the dental health of his patients. He has played a key role contributing towards successful growth of Toothpix Dental healthcare ever since its inception.

Born and brought up in the beautiful coastal town of Mangalore, Dr.Shivaram Panya graduated from the esteemed A B Shetty Memorial Institute of Dental Sciences and completed his masters from Coorg Institute of Dental Sciences. Specialising in Clinical Dentistry, Dental Surgery and LASER dentistry, he is a definite asset to the team at Toothpix Dental Healthcare. His expertise lies in the area of gum health (Periodontology) and Implantology, which has contributed a lot toward the growth of our dental clinic being able to offer comprehensive dental services.

This silent, confident, and ever-smiling doctor is a huge cricket fan and his keen interest in technology makes him the main contributor towards the technical advancement at Toothpix Dental Healthcare.
